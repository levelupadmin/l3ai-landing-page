You can take just Foundations, the first four months, and walk out with a real MVP. Most people go the full six because that is where you ship something live. We help you choose when you apply.

Is this actually live, or recorded? +

Live. Capped at 40, with mentors, on weekends. We do not sell recordings as live. Everything is recorded for catch-up, but the building happens together.

I run a business. Will this fit my schedule? +

It is built for working people. Classes are on weekends, with optional weekday help if you get stuck. Plan for about 8 to 10 hours a week, including your own building.

Do I need an idea to start? +

No. Some people arrive with one, some find it in the first month. Either way you build on something real to you.

Yes. It is there to filter out people who won't show up. If we don't take you, you get it back.
