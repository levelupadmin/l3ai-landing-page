Module 01AI Fluency+

Stop getting average answers from AI. Learn how these models actually work and how to make them produce work you can rely on.

Learn

How LLMs work (tokens, context windows, hallucination) · the four prompt patterns · system prompts, personas, structured output · chain-of-thought reasoning · grounded research on your own documents · model selection (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, NotebookLM).

You build

A reusable prompt library and a research assistant grounded in your own documents.

Module 02Generative AI & Content+

Go from text to full creative production. Direct AI to produce professional images, video and voice, then wire it into a content system that runs weekly.

Learn

Image models (prompt structure, styles, references, composition) · visual identity, brand kits, templates · AI video scripting and assembly · voice cloning and narration · content systems and calendars · tools: Midjourney, Ideogram, Canva AI, Runway, Google Veo, ElevenLabs, Descript.

You build

A brand kit, an AI-made promo video and a content engine producing a week of real output.

Module 03Automation+

Make your work run itself. Map real processes and turn them into multi-step automations connected across the tools you already use.

Learn

The trigger-action model · mapping business processes · Make and Zapier · n8n fundamentals · APIs, webhooks and MCP in plain terms · debugging and error handling.

You build

Live automations running on your actual tools, connected through real APIs.

Module 04AI Agents & No-Code Products+

The month you cross from user to builder. Build agents that finish real jobs on their own, then wrap them into a working product without writing code.

Learn

Agent anatomy (goal, tools, memory, loop) · agents vs automations, single vs multi-agent · real data and human-in-the-loop · reliability and testing · product thinking, no-code apps, auth and data basics · tools: Relevance AI, n8n agents, custom GPTs, Lovable, Bubble, v0, Supabase.

You build

A working agent doing a recurring job, inside a no-code app you built around it.

Module 05Build Your MVP on Real DataFoundations finish line · Pro entry point+

Turn everything into one real product. Spec it, build it with AI coding tools, connect real data, launch it and put it in front of first users at Demo Day.

Learn

Product specs and scoping · vibe coding with Claude Code, Codex, Cursor · applied RAG · data handling and auth · positioning, landing pages, validation · tools: Cursor, Supabase, vector stores, landing builders, analytics.

You build

A working MVP on real data, launched and presented at Demo Day.

Module 06Production & Deployment+

The depth every other program skips. Take your MVP to production grade and deploy it live for real users, with the engineering to prove it works.

Learn

LLM APIs in real code (Python, OpenAI and Anthropic SDKs, Git) · RAG at scale (embeddings, vector DBs, chunking, hybrid search) · multi-agent systems (LangGraph) · evaluation suites · governance, guardrails, safety, cost control · deployment and operations (Vercel, cloud, monitoring) plus a senior code review.

You build

A deployed, monitored, production-grade product with a live URL, presented at the Final Demo Day.

Questions

The things people ask first.

I'm not technical. I've genuinely never built anything. +

That's exactly who Foundations and Mastery are for. They start at zero. If you can use ChatGPT, you can start.

Is this actually live, or a recording mill? +

100% live and weekend-led. Recordings exist only for catch-up. We'll never sell you a recording as “live.”

How much time does it take every week? +

Around 8–10 hours: two live weekend sessions, optional weekday doubt clinics, and your own build time.

Will this get me a job, a raise, a promotion? +

No promises, and we won't imply any. You leave with capability and a portfolio of real, shipped work. What you do with it is yours.

Isn't all of this free on YouTube? +

The information is. The structure isn't: a deadline, feedback on your actual build, and a room that expects your demo on Sunday. You're paying to actually build.

₹90,000 is a lot. What if I join and it's not for me? +

That's what the selection exists to prevent. We read your application and interview you before any tuition moves. If we think you won't get value, we say no and tell you why.

How do I know this works? +

Everything is checkable before you apply: the curriculum, the fee, the mentors. And the output is public. Every builder demos a working MVP and a deployed product in front of the room and invited guests.

Can I start with Foundations and continue later? +

Yes. Pro picks up exactly where Foundations stops. Bought separately the two cost ₹1,30,000; Mastery is the same journey for ₹90,000. The saving is for deciding once.

Can I pay in installments? +

Yes. No-cost EMI on every pathway, with the monthly amount, tenure and total shown together. Mastery also has an in-house two-part split.

Is the ₹700 application fee really refundable? +

Yes. Returned if we say no, credited to your tuition if you're in. It exists to filter out the casual, not to make money.
